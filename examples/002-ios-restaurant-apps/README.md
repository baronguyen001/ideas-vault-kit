# 002 - iOS Apps for Local Restaurants

> Public claim: build iOS apps for local restaurants quickly with AI, charge a one-time setup fee, and repeat the process.

**Evaluation date**: 2026-05-28
**Verdict**: **NO-GO**
**Adjusted score**: **14/40**

---

## TL;DR

- The "30-minute app" claim confuses prototype generation with a submitted, approved, maintained restaurant app.
- Apple App Store Guideline 4.2.6 is the main blocker: template apps must be submitted by the content provider, not a generic app factory.
- The restaurant ordering market is saturated with POS suites, ordering widgets, and done-for-you website products.
- The example founder profile is technical and automation-leaning, but this idea is mostly local B2B sales, onboarding, and support.
- Better pivots keep the SMB automation insight but remove App Store policy risk.
