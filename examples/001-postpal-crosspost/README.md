## TL;DR

- Indie founders already pay for scheduling and writing tools, but many dislike heavy social suites.
- The wedge is narrow: markdown-first cross-posting for technical builders who want draft control, link hygiene, and platform-specific previews.
- The MVP is feasible with OAuth, queueing, markdown transforms, and a small scheduler.
- Competition exists, which validates demand, but PostPal should avoid becoming a generic Buffer clone.
- Kill it if 50 target users do not convert at least 10% to a waitlist after a focused demo.
