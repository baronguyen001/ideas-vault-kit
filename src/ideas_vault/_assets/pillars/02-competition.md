# Competition Calibration

Use this pillar to score market validation, competitor pressure, and the clarity of your niche carve-out.

| Score | What it looks like |
|---|---|
| 9-10 | Demand is validated, competitors exist but none own the niche, pricing is proven, and your wedge is obvious in one sentence. |
| 7-8 | A real market exists with several competitors. You can name a narrow persona, channel, or workflow where incumbents are weak. |
| 5-6 | Customers seem to care, but competition is broad or substitutes are strong. The niche is plausible but not yet sharp. |
| 3-4 | Crowded or saturated market, weak differentiation, low trust, price compression, or a distribution advantage held by incumbents. |
| 0-2 | Dominated by a platform or winner, no clear demand, or no credible path to reach buyers. |

Market-status modifier:

| Status | Modifier | Meaning |
|---|---:|---|
| blue_ocean | -2 | Demand is not yet validated. |
| validated | +2 | 3-7 competitors, demand exists, no dominant winner. |
| crowded | 0 | 10+ competitors, niche required. |
| saturated | -3 | 50+ competitors or commodity pricing. |
| dominated | -5 | One or two winners own most of the market. |
