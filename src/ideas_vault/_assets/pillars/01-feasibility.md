# Feasibility Calibration

Use this pillar to score build difficulty, policy risk, and whether one person can ship the first useful version.

| Score | What it looks like |
|---|---|
| 9-10 | MVP can ship in days, uses boring tools, no special approval, no hard data dependency, first user can be onboarded by the founder. |
| 7-8 | Build is straightforward but has one integration, payment, deployment, or onboarding wrinkle. Timeline is weeks, not months. |
| 5-6 | Feasible, but scope is easy to underestimate. Multiple integrations, quality bars, or operational steps can stretch the build. |
| 3-4 | Real blockers exist: platform review risk, complex compliance, hard onboarding, or a technical area the founder has not shipped before. |
| 0-2 | Near-deal-breaker: illegal, blocked by platform policy, impossible without proprietary data, or too large for one person. |

Red flags that force a score <=2:

- The core workflow violates a platform policy or law.
- The product is only useful after a large dataset, marketplace, or partner network exists.
- A regulated license is required before the first paying customer.
